Burrito

Description

It's a... burrito.

Ingredients

  • about 2 hamburgers worth ground beef
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 small onion
  • few cloves garlic (or more)
  • 1 jalepeno (or hot chili)
  • rice (make a cup!)
  • cheese of choice
  • lettuce
  • tortillas
  • plain oil
  • salt & pepper
  • cayenne
  • paprika
  • cumin
  • oregano

Steps

  1. START THE RICE. Don't be like me and forget until the end. For the love of God use a rice cooker it's faster and better texture.
  2. Chop onion and bell pepper into strips. Heat pan and add oil. Cook on medium heat so they start to brown.
  3. In another pan, add ground beef and minced garlic.
  4. Once ground beef is mostly brown, I drain some of the grease. Add minced jalepeno and spices. I measure with my heart. But a teaspoon to tablespoon for each. Careful with the salt.
  5. Add beans and some water. I cover and simmer, stirring every few minutes. I want it a little mushy.
  6. Assemble! I nuke my tortillas and some cheese in the microwave so they get warm. I like some kewpie mayo too.
