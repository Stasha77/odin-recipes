Creamy Shin Ramen

Description

My strange addiction: shin ramen edition

Ingredients

  • 1 pack Shin Ramen
  • Kewpie Mayo
  • cheese (american preferable)
  • few cloves garlic
  • green onion as wanted

Steps

  1. mince and saute garlic in medium pan
  2. add about half water called for on Shin packet and bring to boil
  3. add packets and noodles and cover. Flip noodles after about a minute so they cook evenly.
  4. uncover and cook off water until only a few tablespoons is left. Near the end add the green onion and cheese so it begins to melt.
  5. move to bowl. Add healthy amount of kewpie mayo (1-2 tablespoons) and stir well. Enjoy!
