Toad In The Hole

Description

Why cook your eggs and toast seperately?

Efficiency at it's finest.

*also called Eggs In The Basket

Ingredients (for one toad in the hole)

  • 1 egg
  • 1 slice sandwich bread
  • couple tablespoons butter
  • salt (as wanted)

Steps

  1. While pan heats (medium-heat), use a shot glass to cut small circle out of center of the bread.
  2. Add some butter and begin toasting one side of bread.
  3. Flip bread and put more butter in center. Crack the egg into center.
  4. Once egg white is cooked enough so that it holds, gently flip bread. Careful not to break the yolk!
  5. Cook egg whites on other side. This is fast. You want the egg yolk to still be runny.
  6. Slide onto plate, sprinkle salt, enjoy!
